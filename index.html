Projects

Yellow taxi trips: From ETL pipeline, to analytics and machine learning

A company wants to invest in the Transportation field, they want to provide transportation services as Uber or DiDi. But first, they need to have a solid frame of reference to make good investment desicions. This is when we come in, analyzing 3.5 Million of records of yellow taxi trips of March 2022 to provide them with the necesary information of the movement in the business. We created the ETL Pipeline in AWS, then a relational database in redshift that we connected to Power BI to create the dashboards and finally, we created a machine learning model in the streamlit interface.
